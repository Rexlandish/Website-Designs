About Derren Niceplace, CEO

Derren Niceplace is the 5th CEO of Niceplace Park
What are his goals?

Togetherness

Derren believes that togetherness is paramount for a thriving community. He has taken multiple steps to ensure that the park fosters a welcoming feeling to all.

Belongingness

Derren has introduced many areas where locals can express their individuality. These include a graffiti wall, and public stage open to everyone to perform.
